How To Add Us To Your Email ?

Instructions – to add us to your email - How can I add P&G to my email Safe Sender List?

  To make sure you receive our emails please add the domain name of our email provider to your safe sender list (sometimes called a whitelist).

In addition, if you find email from P&G in your spam or junk folder, you will need to tell your email software that it's not spam by hitting the not spam or not junk button. This action will train your email program to allow emails from these addresses in the future.

GMail

To ensure that you receive our emails in your inbox, you can add the last part of our email address (called the domain name) to your contact list. Log into your GMail account

  • Click the Show search options downward-pointing triangle in your Gmail main search box in the middle of the page.
  • Add "Salesforce.com" into the box titled ‘Has the words’ (not including the quotation marks)
  • Click on ‘Create filter with this search’ link at the bottom of the search window.
  • Make sure 'Never send it to Spam' is checked.
  • Click Create filters

Outlook.com (Formerly Hotmail or MSN)

Open your Outlook mailbox.

  • Select Options from the top right (next to the question mark)
  • Select Safe and blocked senders (under Preventing junk email) >Safe senders.
  • In the space provided, enter "@Salesforce.com"
  • Select Add to list.

Yahoo

Log on to your Yahoo account

  • Click on the [Addresses] or [contacts] icon (top left hand side of the page)
  • Click on [Add Contact]
  • In the Name section add 'P&G' and with Email you should add '@salesforce.com’
  • Click [Save Contact] and then on the next page click [Done]

Outlook

Log into your Outlook account

  • From the menu bar select "Actions", then "Junk E-mail", then "Junk E-mail Options"
  • From the "Junk E-mail Options" window select the "Safe Recipients" tab
  • Click the [Add] button
  • In the "Add Address or Domain" window enter '@salesforce.com’ in the text entry box
  • Click the [OK] button on Add Domain box and then click the next [OK] button on the Junk Email Options box.

Most Other Email Clients

   Normally it is simply a case of adding "Salesforce.com" as an email address to your Contact list and/or Address book. Once you have "Salesforce.com" registered as a genuine contact, your email shouldn’t consider any correspondence from us as spam. Search the 'Help' section

Please note that it is possible that not all emails from Salesforce.com will be delivered to your inbox, there may be cases where it is delivered to your bulk/junk mail folders. Please ensure that you check them regularly to ensure that you do not miss any emails from P&G.

Do you have any studies I could participate in soon?

   We have a variety of studies and each study has unique qualifications based on the project team needs. We will email you study questionnaires for upcoming studies. The answers you provide determine your eligibility.

If you qualify, we will contact you either via email or phone with the full study details. Be sure to check your email often.

What kind of studies do you have coming up?

   We never know what’s around the corner, but we always have a variety of studies running from product testing in a facility or in your home, and discussions about products you are using.

We send out questionnaires for all our upcoming studies. Check your email often.

Does this study pay more or less than the other one I just filled out?

   We receive panel and payment details once a panelist qualifies. You may qualify for more than one study and be paid for both studies. If you qualify, we will contact you either via email or phone with the full study details and payment information.

Compensation is based on your time, travel, and effort required for each panel. Each panel requires different levels of effort and time.

When do I get paid?

  It normally takes 10-15 business days to receive compensation after you have completed a study.

If you do not receive your payment within this time frame, please contact us at 513-242-5752 so we may inquire about the status on your behalf.

I forgot what time my appointment is scheduled. How do I find out?

If you qualify, we will contact you either via email or phone with the full study details.

If I bring a friend, can she do the study as well?

  No, all our studies require specific qualifications and not everyone qualifies for every study.

Your friend can register on our home page to become a panelist for future studies.

Why haven’t I been called yet?

  Qualifications vary on every study. If you have not been contacted via email or phone from us, then you did not qualify for that specific study.

We run hundreds of studies a year so be sure to check your email often for the next opportunity.

I just did a study and they said two weeks to get my bank card, it’s been two weeks today and it didn’t come?

  You will receive your Pre-Paid Visa card between 10-15 business days (excluding holidays) after you have completed a panel.

If you haven’t received your payment, please call us at 513-242-5752 so we may investigate the issue.

Please remember, your Pre-Paid Visa card is valid for 6 months after issuance and does not have ATM access.

Expired Pre-Paid Visa cards cannot be reissued and are not reloadable at this time.

I just got an Email that said I qualify for a study?

  Wonderful, that means you have met the qualifications required for the study and we look forward to seeing you participate.

If you have any specific questions about your confirmation email, please call us at 513-242-5752 .

Can I bring my kids? / Do you have child care?

  Children are not permitted to attend the research with you nor can you leave any children in a waiting area. If you arrive with any children, you will not be able to participate and there will be no compensation.

** Note the only exception is for panelists who are either picking up or dropping off product.

The majority of the research facilities do not provide child care. If we are working with a facility providing child care, you will be informed in your email confirmation or via phone.

Still have questions? Call us at 513-242-5752.

Can I finish the survey using my cell phone?

  Yes, you can access surveys on any device (smart phone, tablet, computer) that has an internet browser.

I filled the questionnaire /screener out wrong, can you resend it?

  Unfortunately, your answers were captured and the answers you provided disqualified you from qualifying. To keep the integrating of the data, we do not allow repeated attempts to qualify for a study.
